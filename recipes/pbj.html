Peanut Butter Jelly

Description

This is a sandwich filled with peanut butter and jelly of a flavor or your choice. It is a great snack that can be made in about 5-10 minutes.

Ingredient List

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Butter

Recipe

  1. Apply some butter two the two sides of two slices of bread
  2. Toast these two slices on a pan till it slightly browns and becomes crispy
  3. Apply peanut butter to one side of one slice of bread
  4. Apply jelly to one side of one slice of bread
  5. Put the two sides together
  6. Enjoy your delicious peanut butter and jelly sandwich
